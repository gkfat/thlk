Boris Kao, Attorney at Law

Boris Kao’s practice is focused on financial transaction dispute, cross-border investment, merger & acquisition and capital market. Mr. Kao is experienced in handling financial cases involving the Company Act and the Securities and Exchange Act as well as disputes of derivative transactions such as futures trading. Mr. Kao provides legal services including, among other things, conducting legal due diligence, drafting and reviewing contracts and transaction documents as well as offering corporate legal advice. Mr. Kao also handles governmental filings with Financial Supervisory Commission (FSC) and Investment Commission of Ministry of Economic Affairs (MOEAIC).

Mr. Kao obtained his Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.) degree from College of Law, National Chung-Cheng University, and he is admitted to practice in Taiwan.

  • Professional Qualifications: Member of Taipei Bar Association
