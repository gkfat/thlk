Marx Lai, Partner

Marx Lai is an experienced corporate lawyer with specialties in mergers & acquisitions, securities exchange, competition laws and commercial litigation. Mr. Lai has participated in many major mergers & acquisitions transactions in Taiwan and provided comprehensive services including due diligence, project structuring, application documents and contracts drafting, negotiations and delivering legal opinions. In competition laws area, Mr. Lai has extensive experience obtaining antitrust clearances in Taiwan in relation to large cross-border transactions for clients from various industries, such as telecommunication, luxury products, high-end technologies and financial institutions. Mr. Lai also advised clients on transaction structuring from a perspective of competition laws and assisted his clients with regulatory investigation procedures by the Taiwan FTC.

Prior to joining THLK Partners, Mr. Lai practiced law at LCS & Partners, where he was a senior attorney-at-laws in the firm’s corporate law department specializing in Fair Trade Law, mergers & acquisitions, contracts drafting and commercial litigation. Mr. Lai obtained both his Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.) and Master of Laws (LL.M.) degrees from College of Law, National Taiwan University. Later, Mr. Lai was granted a government fellowship for oversea study by Ministry of Education, Taiwan, and received his another Master of Laws degree and Certificate in Business Administration (LL.M/Kellogg) from School of Law and Kellogg School of Management, Northwestern University. Mr. Lai is admitted to practice in Taiwan and State of New York, USA. Mr. Lai is also a member of Taipei Bar Association’s Competition Laws Committee and acts as an adjunct instructor at Taiwan Academy of Banking and Finance, teaching M&A and Fair Trade Law.
