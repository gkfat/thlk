Private Equity

Private equities have been developed for different transactional purposes and investment targets, e.g., venture capital investments, mergers & acquisitions, publicly traded securities investments, real estate investments, intellectual property acquisitions, art investments, and film productions. To meet different needs of clients, private equities can also be structured in various legal formats, such as in forms of trusts, contracts, partnerships or companies. Each type of legal structures addresses clients' different concerns and requirements.

THLK Partners provides the following legal services in the area of private equity:

  • Private equity fund structuring
  • Investment vehicle establishment and governance document drafting
  • Investor agreement negotiation and drafting
  • Management agreement negotiation and drafting
  • Offering document drafting
  • Regulatory filings
  • Advisory services regarding private equity laws and regulations
