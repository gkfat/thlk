Sean Tung, Partner

Sean Tung is a well experienced lawyer with specialties in civil, criminal, and administrative litigations. As a leading litigator in these traditional areas, Mr. Tung has also represented clients in complicated commercial litigations involving the Business Entity Accounting Law, the Company Act, the Securities Exchange Act and the Securities Investment Trust and Consulting Act. Mr. Tung has successfully defended his clients against numerous wrongfully charged insider trading, stock price manipulation and false financial statement cases. Mr. Tung also specializes in the sector of real estate practice. Mr. Tung has successfully represented clients in various contentious or non-contentious cases regarding real estate transactions, commercial leases, real estate brokerages, co-development, trust and urban redevelopments. With his solid knowledge and experiences in real estate foreclosure sales and compulsory enforcement procedures, Mr. Tung has been recruited as an adjunct instructor of Compulsory Enforcement Law for many years. Mr. Tung’s clients include listing real estate developers, well-known real estate brokerage firms, and individual investors.

Prior to joining THLK Partners, Mr. Tung had been a senior litigator at Formosa Transitional Attorneys at Law, and a counsel at LCS & Partners, focusing on civil, criminal, administrative, commercial and securities law litigations. Mr. Tung obtained both his Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.) and Master of Laws (LL.M.) degrees from College of Law, National Taiwan University. Mr. Tung is admitted to practice in Taiwan. He is also a lecturer at Taiwan Academy of Banking and Finance (TABF).
