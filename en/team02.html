Eric Hsu, Partner

Eric Hsu specializes in derivative transaction disputes, project financing, securitization, real estate and inbound and outbound investments. For the derivative transaction disputes, Mr. Hsu represented a dozen of Taiwanese small-and-medium size enterprises in dealing with their target redemption forward (TRF) disputes with banks, with aggregate settlement amount of tens of millions of US dollars. For project financing, Mr. Hsu was responsible for several cross-border deals in the total amount of more than 100 million US dollars. In the securitization area, Mr. Hsu participated in a dozen of securitization deals of various types in Taiwan (including CMBS, REITs, CLO, CBO, ABCP, etc.) and provided comprehensive services including conducting legal due diligence, arranging transaction structure, preparing application/reporting documents and delivering legal opinions. In the real estate area, Mr. Hsu represented prestigious domestic financial institutions in several commercial real estate bid sales and real estate co-development projects and assisted the clients with contract drafting and negotiation. With respect to inbound and outbound investments, Mr. Hsu represented Labor Insurance Fund, Labor Pension Fund and several prestigious domestic financial institutions to deal with matters regarding their respective overseas investments (particularly derivatives transactions).

Prior to joining THLK Partners, Mr. Hsu headed the Legal and Compliance Department as well as the Risk and Capital Committee at PineBridge Investments Taiwan for some years, and thus he has extensive practical experience and knowledge in the day-to-day business operation of an asset management firm (particularly the offering and distribution of onshore and offshore funds). Prior to his tenure at PineBridge Investments, Mr. Hsu practiced law at LCS & Partners, where he was senior associate with a primary focus on securitization, real estate and inbound and outbound investments. Mr. Hsu obtained his Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.) degree from School of Law, National Taiwan University. Mr. Hsu is admitted to practice in Taiwan.
