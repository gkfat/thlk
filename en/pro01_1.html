Capital Markets

Capital market has always been the major tool of funding and exit strategies for companies and strategic investors. Nonetheless, companies and investors often have different goals and concerns when approaching capital markets. Hence, an experienced and thoughtful corporate lawyer, with support from accountants and financial advisors, can often prevent backfires beforehand by tailor-made structuring to meet client goals and mitigate associated risks.

Our lawyers successfully assisted enterprises and financial institutions in domestic and global offerings, and had in-house experiences with transnational companies. THLK Partners provides the following legal services in the area of capital market:

  • Tailor-made deal structuring
  • GDR, ADR and TDR offering and listing
  • ECB and CB offering and listing
  • Taiwan Stock Exchange and GreTai Securities Market listing
  • Advisory services regarding capital market laws and regulations
