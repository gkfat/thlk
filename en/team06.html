Liangju Chiu, Junior Partner

Liangju Chiu has extensive practical experience in contentious matters as he has represented various clients in civil, criminal, administrative and intellectual property litigations as well as compulsory enforcement proceedings and family matters. Mr. Chiu provides comprehensive services including general legal consultation, contract drafting and reviewing, compliance checking as well as legal due diligence.

Prior to joining THLK Partners, Mr. Chiu served at the Litigation Counselling Section of Taiwan Miaoli District Court, where he provided legal counselling services to laypeople. Throughout his career, Mr. Chiu has contributed to local communities by continuously offering pro-bono legal services to disadvantaged groups. Mr. Chiu obtained his Master of Laws (LL. M.) degree from Program in Technology Law of Department of Law, National Chung Hsing University, with a master thesis titled “Patent Eligibility of Software Related Inventions in Light of the Alice v. CLS Bank Decision,” and he has authored numerous articles relating to laws on intellectual property rights.
