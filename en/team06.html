Liangju Chiu, Junior Partner

Liangju Chiu has yearslong and extensive practical experience in contentious matters as he has represented various clients in civil, criminal, administrative and intellectual property litigations as well as compulsory enforcement proceedings and family matters. Mr. Chiu provides comprehensive services including general legal consultation, contract drafting and reviewing, compliance checking as well as legal due diligence.
