Corporate Governance

Corporate governance relates to Company Law, Securities Exchange Act and many other laws and regulations. If managers' business decisions are at odds with relevant laws and regulations, the managers would be exposed to the risks of incurring civil, administrative and even criminal liabilities. Hence, professional advices provided by corporate lawyers can help managers of a company mitigate such risks.

In addition, establishing an optimal internal framework is vital for any company seeking to deal effectively with risks that arise in the day-to-day business activities and to promote ongoing corporate expansion. Through the cooperation with CPAs and other professionals, the experienced corporate lawyers at THLK Partners can help our clients establish a sound internal control and management system which prevents acts of misconduct.

On the other hand, THLK Partners offers legal services dealing with matters that arise in the course of the client's day-to-day business activities, such as contract drafting and negotiations. Our lawyers use know-how gained from acting as external or internal legal counsel to a wide array of corporations over the years, offering support for routine business operations as well as novel legal issues that a client may encounter.

THLK Partners provides the following services in the area of corporate governance:

  • Providing legal opinions to corporate clients' day-to-day operation and transactions
  • Assisting corporate clients to communicate with competent authorities
  • Helping corporate clients establish internal control and audit system in compliance with relevant laws and regulations
  • Drafting all kinds of commercial contracts and participating in the negotiation with the counterparty of contracts
